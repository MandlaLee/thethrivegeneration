Our Leadership Standard

Character before title.

Leadership at Kingship is not built around status, control or public visibility. Leaders are expected to model humility, accountability, discipline, honour, consistency and genuine care for people.

Healthy leadership produces healthy culture, and healthy culture creates communities where people can grow safely and sustainably.

Leadership Commitments

How we choose to lead.

01

Serve first

Leadership begins with responsibility, availability and care for people.

02

Protect the culture

We guard spiritual health, biblical truth, honour and unity.

03

Develop others

Strong leaders create opportunities for others to learn, serve and grow.

04

Remain accountable

Authority must always be accompanied by transparency, wisdom and correction.
