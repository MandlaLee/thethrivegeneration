The Thrive Generation

Building people who walk with Christ and influence their generation.

Kingship Christian Centre is a Christ-centred community formed to help believers grow spiritually, pursue purpose, build meaningful relationships and carry the nature of Christ into every environment.

Why Kingship Exists

The narrow road was never meant to be walked alone.

Many believers understand the call to live differently, yet pressure, distraction, temptation and cultural influence can make that journey feel isolating. Kingship Christian Centre was formed in response to this reality.

We are creating a community where people can remain devoted to God while being surrounded by encouragement, accountability, mentorship and genuine connection. Our goal is not to withdraw from culture, but to influence it through lives rooted in Christ.

At our core

We are not simply building events, departments or programmes. We are building people—disciples who understand spiritual responsibility, emotional health, leadership, excellence and cultural influence.

Founder & Lead Pastor

Pastor Jasper Samuel

Pastor, husband, father, author, mentor and kingdom leader

Jasper Samuel is devoted to helping individuals and families pursue spiritual growth, emotional healing and healthy covenant relationships. His ministry journey includes prophetic and apostolic service, counselling, writing, leadership development and the practical experience of building family and business foundations.

Born in Nigeria, he later relocated to South Africa in 2011 in response to a growing spiritual calling. After seasons of faithful service and development, he founded Kingship Christian Centre in Midrand in 2026 with a focus on identity, transformation, biblical truth, healing, leadership and kingdom living.

Healthy influence must first be built on private transformation.
Meet the Leadership Team

Jasper found Christ at an early age, walked with God through childhood, pastored during his school years and developed musically as part of his spiritual formation. These years shaped his commitment to worship, teaching and pastoral care.

While serving at Global Faith Ministries in East London, he grew in leadership, counselling and discipleship. In 2019, he moved to Bloemfontein, where ministry, family and business experiences deepened his understanding of people and covenant relationships.

His books and teaching combine biblical principles, pastoral wisdom and emotional insight to guide people through relationships, forgiveness, healing and personal transformation.

Jasper views work as worship and stewardship. His approach to business emphasises integrity, servant leadership and using marketplace influence to advance God’s purposes rather than profit alone.
