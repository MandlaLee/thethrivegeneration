GIVING QUESTIONS

Frequently asked questions

Can I choose what my contribution supports?

Yes. Use your payment reference or contact the church to indicate a specific ministry, programme, or outreach project.

Can I partner monthly?

Yes. Recurring partnership options will be confirmed once the church banking and payment systems are finalised.

Can I donate equipment or supplies?

Yes. Contact the centre before delivering goods so the team can confirm current needs and receiving arrangements.

Will I receive confirmation?

Once the giving system is connected, confirmation and record-keeping procedures can be added. For now, send proof of payment through WhatsApp or email.
