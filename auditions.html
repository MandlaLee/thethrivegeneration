What We Value

Gifted people with the character to grow.

Auditions are not only about ability. Kingship Worship is looking for people who value worship, humility, spiritual growth, preparation, teamwork and consistency.

01

Spiritual Depth

Public worship should flow from a genuine and growing relationship with God.

02

Teachability

Every team member should remain open to training, correction, mentorship and development.

03

Commitment

Rehearsals, communication, preparation and reliability are important parts of serving well.

04

Excellence

Excellence is not performance or pride. It is the discipline of honouring God by giving our best.
