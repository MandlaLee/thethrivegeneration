Why We Exist

Raise people. Restore lives. Influence culture through Christ.

Our vision and mission describe the kind of people we are committed to building and the environments we are called to create.

Our Vision

A generation under the authority of Christ.

To raise a generation of kingdom-minded leaders who live under the authority of Christ and influence culture through worship, excellence, leadership and purpose.

This vision is not limited to producing church members. We desire to develop disciples and leaders who carry spiritual depth, wisdom, integrity and influence.

Faith should shape how believers think, speak, create, study, work, lead and build relationships. We are raising people who influence the world without being controlled by it.

Our Mission

Build believers spiritually before positioning them publicly.

Our mission is to disciple people, cultivate a strong worship and prayer culture, restore relationships, reach campuses and create environments of purpose, growth and excellence rooted in Christ.

1

Disciple intentionally

Teach Scripture, prayer, accountability, character and spiritual discipline.

2

Cultivate worship

Raise worshippers marked by spiritual authenticity, humility and excellence.

3

Build prayer altars

Strengthen intercession, fasting, revival and personal devotion.

4

Restore relationships

Support singles, couples and families with biblical guidance and healing.

5

Reach campuses

Equip students to remain grounded and influence educational spaces.

6

Develop purpose and leadership

Create opportunities to learn, serve, create, lead and grow responsibly.

What Guides Us

Our ministry convictions.

These statements are concise public-facing beliefs drawn from the current church material. A formal doctrinal statement can be added after pastoral approval.

Christ is the centre

True fulfilment, identity and purpose are found in Jesus Christ and submission to His authority.

Scripture shapes life

Biblical truth guides spiritual growth, leadership, relationships, service and cultural engagement.

Prayer sustains ministry

Every pillar stands on prayer, intercession, fasting and dependence on God.

Worship is a lifestyle

Worship extends beyond music into surrender, obedience, humility and devotion.

People are developed intentionally

Transformation requires teaching, mentorship, accountability and healthy community.

Believers should influence society

Faith belongs in campuses, workplaces, creative industries, families and leadership spaces.

The Result We Seek

Transformation in people, not numbers alone.

We are building spiritually mature, emotionally healthy, purpose-driven and culturally impactful believers who carry the presence and character of Christ wherever they go.

Find Your Place

Grow through one of our four ministries.

UniThrive, The Prayer Ministry, Thrive In Two and Kingship Worship each provide a pathway to encounter God, grow and serve.
