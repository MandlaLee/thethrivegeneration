OPEN HANDS PROGRAMME

Serving people with compassion, dignity, and practical support.

Open Hands is the community-care expression of Kingship Christian Centre, created to meet practical needs while reflecting the love of Christ.

WHY OPEN HANDS EXISTS

Faith expressed through action

Open Hands supports individuals, families, students, and communities through organised outreach and practical assistance. The programme is designed to serve responsibly, protect dignity, and direct resources toward genuine needs.

Programmes shown on this page are placeholders based on the church’s emerging outreach vision. Final dates, beneficiaries, collection procedures, and distribution partners should be confirmed before public launch.

CURRENT NEEDS

Items and support we may receive

  • Non-perishable food
  • School uniforms and stationery
  • Clean clothing and blankets
  • Hygiene products
  • Transport and logistics assistance
  • Professional services and referrals
  • Financial sponsorship for approved outreach projects

Donation standards

Please contact the centre before delivering items. Donations should be clean, safe, usable, appropriately packaged, and suitable for dignified distribution.

We may decline unsuitable or unsafe donations. This protects both recipients and volunteers.
