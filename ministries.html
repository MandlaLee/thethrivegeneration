Our Pillars of Calling

Four ministries.
One kingdom purpose.

At Kingship Christian Centre, we believe God has called us to raise people, restore lives and build Christ-centred communities through four clear expressions of ministry.

The Thrive Generation

Where faith becomes community, purpose and impact.

Every ministry exists to help people encounter God, grow in spiritual maturity, build healthy relationships and carry the nature of Christ into everyday life.

Although each ministry serves a different need, they work together as one family. Students are reached, worshippers are raised, relationships are strengthened and prayer altars are built to sustain revival.

01 / CAMPUS MINISTRY

UniThrive

Meeting students and young adults where they are and guiding them into a deeper relationship with Christ through outreach, Bible studies, mentorship and revival gatherings.

  • Campus outreach and student fellowship
  • Bible teaching and honest conversations
  • Mentorship, accountability and leadership
  • Prayer meetings and revival gatherings
Explore UniThrive
02 / WORSHIP MINISTRY

Kingship Worship

A team of vocalists, musicians and creatives dedicated to Spirit-filled worship, excellent ministry and raising leaders who carry humility, unity and spiritual depth.

  • Worship services and worship nights
  • Music training and songwriting
  • Original music and production
  • Concert support and equipment rental
Explore Kingship Worship
03 / MARRIAGE & RELATIONSHIPS

Thrive In Two

Serving singles, dating couples, engaged couples, married couples and families through biblical teaching, counselling support and practical relationship development.

  • Premarital guidance
  • Couples seminars and workshops
  • Restoration gatherings
  • Family life and counselling support
Explore Thrive In Two
04 / PRAYER MINISTRY

The Prayer Ministry

The engine room of everything God is doing at Kingship Christian Centre, building a culture of intercession, fasting, revival and spiritual breakthrough.

  • Intercession and prayer meetings
  • Prayer room and prayer tours
  • Fasting gatherings
  • Revival services and personal ministry
Explore The Prayer Ministry
