Who We Are

We are not simply building programmes. We are building people.

Many believers desire to live for God, yet feel isolated by pressure, distraction and a culture pulling them in another direction. Kingship Christian Centre exists to create a community where people can pursue Christ without walking alone.

Through discipleship, worship, prayer, mentorship and intentional relationships, we are raising people who remain spiritually grounded while growing in excellence, confidence and purpose.

Meet Our Lead Pastor

Founder, Lead Pastor, Author and Kingdom Leader

His ministry carries a passion for discipleship, leadership development, biblical truth and building people who can influence society while remaining fully submitted to Christ.
