Volunteer

Kingship is preparing for launch, and strong ministry is built by people who serve with humility, consistency, spiritual maturity and excellence.

A Culture of Service

Use your gifts to help build people.

Volunteering is not about titles or visibility. It is an opportunity to serve Christ, support people and help create healthy environments where ministry can flourish.

Applications are open for people who are teachable, dependable and willing to grow within the church’s culture and leadership systems.

Ways to Serve

There is room for different gifts.

  • Welcome & Hospitality
    Receive guests and help people feel at home.
  • Prayer & Intercession
    Stand in the gap for people, services and communities.
  • Media & Technology
    Support websites, livestreams, visuals, sound and digital systems.
  • Events & Setup
    Prepare environments with order, safety and excellence.
  • Open Hands Outreach
    Serve practical community needs with compassion.
  • Ministry Support
    Assist UniThrive, Thrive In Two, children and administration.
